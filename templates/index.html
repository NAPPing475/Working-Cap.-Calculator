Calculation Logic

1. Working Capital Gap (WCG): WCG = TCA - OCL
2. Minimum Stipulated Net Working Capital (NWC): NWC = 25% of TCA
3. Maximum Permissible Bank Finance (MPBF): MPBF = (TCA - NWC) * 0.75
4. Drawing Power (DP): DP = MPBF
